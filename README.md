## The Idea

### What we're working on 🚀

**Relatee** is not your ordinary task tracker. It's a dynamic platform that combines household task management with gamification elements, allowing you to turn everyday chores into challenges! 🎮🌟

### Key Features: 💡

- **Task Tracking**: Easily track household tasks and responsibilities, ensuring everything gets done efficiently.
- **Gamification**: Collect points for completing tasks and unlock rewards, adding an element of excitement to your routine.
- **Custom Rewards**: Create and purchase custom rewards tailored to your household's preferences, motivating everyone to contribute.
- **Level System**: Progress through levels as you complete tasks, unlocking new features and enhancing your household's productivity.
